Quick Answers

Frequently Asked

How long does shipping take?

Most orders ship within 1–2 business days from our Chicago warehouse. Standard US shipping takes 3–5 business days. Expedited options are available at checkout.

What is your return policy?

We offer hassle-free returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Contact our team to initiate a return.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes! We work with interior designers, hotels, and boutiques. Use the contact form above and select "Wholesale Inquiry" to get started.

Are your products ethically made?

Absolutely. We work exclusively with makers who meet our standards for fair wages, safe working conditions, and environmental responsibility. Learn more on our About page.
